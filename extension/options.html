Safeguards

These switches gate operations that an LLM consuming attacker-controlled email content could be tricked into performing silently. Disabling them re-enables the corresponding capabilities for MCP clients you trust.

Audit log

Every outbound compose (sendMail, replyToMessage, forwardMessage, saveDraft) and every contact write goes to <profile>/thunderbird-mcp/audit.log as a JSON line. Bodies and attachment contents are not recorded -- only metadata. Newest entries first; the most recent 200 are shown.
